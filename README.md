# TooTMate
A Gamified Study Companion for Students
	
## Introduction
TooTMate is a gamified study companion application that that transforms studying into an interactive and enjoyable experience, much like a role-playing game (RPG). The app incorporates game mechanics such as Experience Points (XP), level progression, achievements, daily and subtask-based challenges, cheerful animations and audio clips, and bonus rewards. These features are designed to encourage students to stay motivated and maintain consistent study habits.
At the heart of TooTMate is the friendly yellow bird mascot, Toots, who guides students throughout their study journey. Toots provides motivational messages, reminders, and progress feedback, and serves as an AI-chatbot companion to support students during burnout, demotivation, or anxiety related to exams and deadlines.

## Objectives
- To design and develop a gamified study application that motivates students to stay consistent in their studies.
- To integrate game mechanics like XP, levels, progress bar, leaderboard and achievements into a productivity tool.
- To help students track and improve their study habits through frequent and interactive progress monitoring.
- To encourage engagement and healthy competition through leaderboards and challenges. 
- To provide a supportive and friendly study environment through Toots the bird.

## Key Features
- Avatar Creation
- Daily Study Tasks and XP System
- Proof Submission and AI Verification
- Daily Check-In and Bonus Rewards
- Level Progression
- Achievements and Milestones
- Leaderboard System and Social Features
- Notifications and Reminders
- Guidance System
